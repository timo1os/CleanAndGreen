Clean & Green

  • Clean and Green is an in initiative school organization in attempt to raise awareness about global warning and pollution and encourage people to recycle.

  • We attempt to raise the community's awareness and understanding of the importance of preserving the environment, showing people a greener approach of throwing out unneeded wastes (for ex: recycling fried cooking oil) rather than polluting the earth soil, and encourage people on regrowing unused wastes in help of reversing climate change (for ex: vegetable scraps, and vegetable seeds).

  • Clean and Green consist of 15 students from MILS school ranging from grade 7 to 8 and our only goal is to save the world 💗.
